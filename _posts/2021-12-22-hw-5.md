4. [불용어(Stopword)](https://wikidocs.net/22530)
5. [정규 표현식(Regular Expression)](https://wikidocs.net/21703)
6. [정수 인코딩(Integer Encoding)](https://wikidocs.net/31766)
7. [패딩(Padding)](https://wikidocs.net/83544)
